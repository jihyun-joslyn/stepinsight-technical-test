Game Rules

How to play

1. Look closely

Each round shows a real-world image. Zoom in and examine details â€” signs, buildings, landscapes, anything can be a clue.

2. Make your guess

Enter the latitude and longitude where you think the photo was taken. There is no map for guessing.

3. Submit your guess

After you submit, we calculate the distance and show a map with your guess, the correct location, and the distance between them.

4. Keep going

Move on to the next round until all 3 rounds are complete.

Scoring

Get within 50 metres of the correct location and you score points. The closer you are, the more points you earn (up to 15).

Your final score is the total from all three rounds.

Game complete

At the end, you will see your final score, each round distance, and a summary. You can play again or return home.
