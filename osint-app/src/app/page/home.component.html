OsInt Challenge

Welcome

Test your OSINT skills by figuring out where in the world each photo was taken. You will play 3 short rounds, and the closer your guess is, the more points you earn!
How to play the game
Look closely Each round shows a real-world image. Zoom in and examine the details â€” signs, buildings, landscapes, anything can be a clue. Make your guess Enter the latitude and longitude where you think the photo was taken. There is no map for guessing, so trust your instincts and research skills. Submit your guess Once you submit your guess: We will calculate how far you are from the answer and you will see a map with your guess, the correct location and the distance between them Keep going Move on to the next round until all 3 rounds are complete Score Get within 50 metres of the correct location and you will score points. The closer you are, the more points you earn. A perfect (or near-perfect) guess can earn up to 15 points. Your final score the total from all three rounds.
Game Complete
At the end You will see your final score, the distance of each round and a summary of each round. You can choose to play a new game at the final page or navigate back to the home screen.
